Our Story

DoubleX Ceramic Coating LLC was founded with one mission: to deliver premium paint protection and detailing services to car enthusiasts and everyday drivers across the Dallas–Fort Worth area.

As a mobile detailing operation, we believe your time is valuable. We bring our professional-grade equipment and products directly to you — whether that's your home, office, or anywhere in DFW. No drop-off, no waiting.

From classic Corvettes to modern supercars, daily drivers to weekend toys, we treat every vehicle with the same obsessive level of care and precision.
